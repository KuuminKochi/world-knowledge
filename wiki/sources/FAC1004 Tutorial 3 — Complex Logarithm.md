# FAC1004 Tutorial 3 — Complex Logarithm

Practice problems on complex logarithms, complex trigonometric functions, and geometric loci.

## Topics Covered

- Complex logarithm (general and principal)
- Complex trigonometric functions
- Geometric interpretations: circles, perpendicular bisectors, half-lines
- Converting between complex and Cartesian equations

## Problem Set

1. **Complex Logarithm**: Find $\ln(z)$ and $\text{Ln}(z)$ (principal value) for:
   - $z = \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{2}i$
   - $z = 2 - \sqrt{3}i$
   - $z = 2\sqrt{3}e^{i\pi/6}$

2. **Complex Trigonometric Functions**:
   - Find Cartesian form of $\sin i$ and $\cos i$
   - Compute $\cos\left(\frac{\pi}{4} - i\right)$ and $\sin\left(\frac{\pi}{4} - i\right)$ using compound angle formulas

3. **Complex Exponential**: Find $\text{Re}(z)$, $\text{Im}(z)$, and $\arg(z)$ for $z = 2^{i+3}$

4. **Circle Loci**: Sketch and find Cartesian equations for:
   - $|z - (4+i)| = 3$
   - $|z - 1 - i| = 5$
   - $|2z + 6 - 4i| = 6$

5. **Perpendicular Bisector Loci**: Sketch and find Cartesian equations for:
   - $|z - (2+i)| = |z - (1+3i)|$
   - $|z + 2 - i| = |z - 1 + 3i|$

6. **Half-line Loci**: Sketch and find Cartesian equations for half-lines at various angles

7. **Combined Problems**: Give both complex and Cartesian equations for:
   - Circle of radius 5 with center at $z = 3 - 2i$
   - Perpendicular bisector of segment connecting $z = -1-2i$ and $z = 3-2i$

8. **Circle from Equation**: Show that $|z - 2| = 2|z + i|$ represents a circle and find its center and radius
