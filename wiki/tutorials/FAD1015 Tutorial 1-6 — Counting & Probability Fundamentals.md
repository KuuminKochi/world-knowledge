## Key Formulas

**Permutations:**
- P(n,r) = n!/(n-r)!
- Circular: (n-1)!
- With repetition: nʳ
- Identical objects: n!/(n₁! × n₂! × ...)

**Conditional Probability:**
- P(A|B) = P(A∩B)/P(B)
- P(A∩B) = P(A|B) × P(B)

**Bayes' Theorem:**
- P(Bᵢ|A) = P(A|Bᵢ) × P(Bᵢ) / Σ P(A|Bⱼ) × P(Bⱼ)

**Expected Value & Variance:**
- E[X] = Σ x·P(X=x)
- Var(X) = E[X²] - (E[X])²
