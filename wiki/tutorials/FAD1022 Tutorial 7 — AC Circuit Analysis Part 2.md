## Question 1

A series [[RLC circuit]] has resistance $R = 12.0 \Omega$, [[Inductive Reactance|inductive reactance]] $X_L = 30.0 \Omega$ and [[Capacitive Reactance|capacitive reactance]] $X_C = 20 \Omega$. If the [[RMS voltage]] across the resistor is 145 V, calculate the:

a) [[Impedance]] — **ans: 15.62 Ω**
b) Voltage across inductor — **ans: 362.4 V**
c) Voltage across capacitor — **ans: 241.6 V**
d) Total voltage supplied — **ans: 188.73 V**
e) [[Average Power|Average power]] delivered to the circuit — **ans: 1752.12 W**
f) [[Reactive Power|Reactive power]] delivered to the circuit — **ans: 1459.2 VAr**
g) [[Apparent Power|Apparent power]] delivered to the circuit — **ans: 2279.37 VA**
h) [[Power Factor|Power factor]] — **ans: 0.77**
i) [[Phase Angle|Phase angle]] between voltage and current — **ans: 39.76°**

Draw [[phasor diagram]] between voltage and current and indicate which signal leads.

## Question 2

A series AC circuit contains a resistor, an inductor of 1.5 H, a capacitor of $5 \mu\text{F}$ and a generator with 240 V, 50 Hz. The current across the circuit is 0.3 A.

**Calculate the:**

a) [[Inductive Reactance|Inductive reactance]] — **ans: 471.24 Ω**
b) [[Capacitive Reactance|Capacitive reactance]] — **ans: 636.62 Ω**
c) [[Impedance]] — **ans: 800 Ω**
d) Resistance of the circuit — **ans: 782.72 Ω**
e) [[Average Power|Average power]], [[Reactive Power|reactive power]] and [[Apparent Power|apparent power]] delivered to the circuit — **ans: 70.45 W, 14.88 VAr, 72 VA**
f) [[Power Factor|Power factor]] — **ans: 0.978**
g) [[Phase Angle|Phase angle]] — **ans: −10.03°**

Draw [[phasor diagram]] between voltage and current and indicate which signal leads. Is the circuit more capacitive or inductive?

## Question 3

Electrosurgical unit (ESUs) supply high frequency electricity from resonant [[RLC circuit]] to cut, coagulate or otherwise modify biological tissue.

**Calculate the:**

a) [[Resonant Frequency|Resonant frequency]] of an ESU with an inductance of $L = 1.25 \mu\text{H}$ and a capacitance of $47.0 \text{ nF}$ — **ans: $6.57 \times 10^5$ Hz**
b) Capacitance required for a [[Resonant Frequency|resonant frequency]] of 1.33 MHz if value of inductance does not change — **ans: $1.146 \times 10^{-8}$ F**
c) [[Phase Angle|Phase angle]] of the ESU at the [[Resonant Frequency|resonant frequency]] — **ans: $\theta = 0°$**
d) [[Power Factor|Power factor]] of the ESU at the [[Resonant Frequency|resonant frequency]] — **ans: PF = 1**

## Additional Question

A series [[RLC circuit]] consists of a resistor ($R=80 \Omega$), an inductor ($L=250\text{mH}$), and a capacitor ($C=10\mu\text{F}$) connected to a variable frequency AC source.

At an unknown frequency, the circuit's [[impedance]] is twice the [[impedance]] at [[Resonance|resonance]]. Given that the [[RMS current]] at [[Resonance|resonance]] is 3 A, determine the [[Resonant Frequency|resonance frequency]] and the possible unknown frequency for this condition.

**ans:** $f_0=100.658 \text{ Hz}$, $f_x = 65.79 \text{ Hz}$ (capacitive circuit) or $154 \text{ Hz}$ (inductive circuit)
