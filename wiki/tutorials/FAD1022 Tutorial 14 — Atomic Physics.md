## Question 4

A sample of a radioactive isotope, Technetium-99m (Tc-99m) is commonly used in medical imaging. A hospital receives a 2.0 µg sample of pure Tc-99m.

**Given information:**
- Half-life of Tc-99m: 6.0 hours
- Mass of Tc-99m: $98.9063 \text{ u}$

**Calculate:**

a) The initial number of Tc-99m atoms ($N_0$) in the 2.0 µg sample
b) The [[decay constant]]
c) The activity after 12 hours (unit must be Bq)
d) How long will it take for the activity of this Tc-99m sample to decay to 10% of its initial activity

## Question 5

**Atomic masses:**
- Uranium-235: $235.04393 \text{ u}$

- Lanthanum-148: $147.921230 \text{ u}$

**Based on the information given:**

a) Write the nuclear reaction and determine the number of protons for La
