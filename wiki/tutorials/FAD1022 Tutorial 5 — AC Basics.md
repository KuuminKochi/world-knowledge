## Question 1

b) value of the unknown resistance, $R_{unknown}$ when the switch is closed and the bridge is brought to equilibrium when variable resistor is 3.0 Ohms.

## Question 5

$$I(t) = 4 \cos \omega t$$

$$V(t) = 10 \sin \omega t$$

**Determine:**

a) The [[phase difference]] between the current and voltage.
b) State clearly which quantity leads and which lags.
c. Draw the [[phasor diagram]] showing the correct phase relationship between current and voltage.
