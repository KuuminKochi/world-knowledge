# FAC1004 Tutorial 2 — Complex Numbers

Practice problems on complex numbers, powers, De Moivre's theorem, and roots.

## Topics Covered

- Powers of complex numbers in polar form
- De Moivre's theorem applications
- Euler's formula and exponential form
- Finding n-th roots of complex numbers
- Solving polynomial equations with complex roots

## Problem Set

1. **Powers in Cartesian Form**: Given $z_1 = -4 + 4i$ and $z_2 = -2\sqrt{3} + 2i$, find $(z_1)^3$ and $(z_2)^4$

2. **Simplification**: Simplify expressions involving $(\cos x + i\sin x)$ terms

3. **Exponential and Cartesian Form**: Express complex numbers in both exponential and Cartesian forms

4. **Argument Calculations**: Calculate arguments and moduli of complex expressions

5. **Euler's Identity Applications**: Show that $e^{ix} + e^{-ix} = 2\cos x$ and related identities

6. **Multiple Angle Formulas**: Prove $\cos(5\theta)$ and $\sin(5\theta)$ formulas

7. **Cube Roots**: Find cube roots of various complex numbers

8. **n-th Roots of Unity**: Find tenth roots of 1 and classify by quadrant

9. **Polynomial Roots**: Find all $z$ for which $z^5 = -32i$ with $\text{Im}(z) > 0$

10. **Complex Polynomial Equations**: Given a root, find coefficients of polynomial

11. **Complex Roots**: Find roots of various complex numbers

12. **Complex Equations**: Solve for $z = x + iy$ in various equations
