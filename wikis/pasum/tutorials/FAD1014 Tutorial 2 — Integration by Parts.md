## Question 1: Integration by Parts Formula

**(a)** $\int x^3 e^{2x} dx$

**(b)** Show that $\int x^m e^{ax} dx = \frac{1}{a}x^m e^{ax} - \frac{m}{a}\int x^{m-1}e^{ax} dx$

Hence, evaluate the following:

**(c)** $\int x^2(e^x - 1)dx$

**(d)** $\int x e^x dx$

## Question 2: Integration Practice

Integrate the following:

**(a)** $\int \ln x \, dx$

**(b)** $\int \ln x^2 \, dx$

**(c)** $\int(\ln x)^2 dx$

**(d)** $\int x^2 \sin x \, dx$

**(e)** $\int e^x \sin 2x \, dx$

**(f)** $\int x^3 e^x dx$
