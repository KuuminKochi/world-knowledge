## Question 1

An elastic metal loop of radius 12.0 cm is placed in a uniform [[magnetic field]] with [[magnetic flux]] density 0.8 T (Figure 1). The loop suddenly shrinks to a radius of 8.0 cm in 0.2 s.

**Calculate:**

a) The change in [[magnetic flux]]
b) Determine the magnitude of induced [[emf]] in the loop
c) State the direction of the induced current
d. Find the magnitude of the induced current if the resistance of the loop is $1.0 \Omega$

**Calculate the average induced [[emf]] in the coil.**
