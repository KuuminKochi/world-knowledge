# FAC1004 — Advanced Mathematics II (Computing)

Foundation-level Advanced Mathematics II course for Computing stream at [[Universiti Malaya]], Centre for Foundation Studies in Science (PASUM).

## Course Description

This course covers advanced mathematical topics tailored for computing students, including complex numbers, inverse trigonometric functions, hyperbolic functions, and additional computing-focused content.

## Topics Covered

### Complex Numbers
- Definition and arithmetic of complex numbers
- Polar and exponential forms (Euler's formula)
- De Moivre's theorem
- n-th roots of complex numbers
- Geometric interpretation (circles, perpendicular bisectors, half-lines)
- Complex logarithms and trigonometric functions

### Inverse Trigonometric Functions
- Definitions, domains, and ranges
- Principal values and graphs
- Properties and identities
- Derivatives of inverse trig functions
- Applications and evaluations

### Hyperbolic Functions
- Definitions in terms of exponentials
- Hyperbolic identities
- Derivatives of hyperbolic functions
- Integrals of hyperbolic functions
- Inverse hyperbolic functions and their logarithmic forms
- Derivatives of inverse hyperbolic functions
- Integrals involving hyperbolic functions

### Computing Applications
- Video project component
- Applications in computing contexts
