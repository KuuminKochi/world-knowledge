# FAD1022 — Basic Physics II

Foundation-level Physics II course at [[Universiti Malaya]], PASUM programme.

## Lecture Modules
- [[FAD1022 L1-L3 — Electrostatics]] — Coulomb's law, electric fields
- [[FAD1022 L7-L9 — Capacitors]] — Dielectrics, combinations, RC circuits
- [[FAD1022 L14-L16 — AC Analysis]] — Alternating current, phasors, reactance
- [[FAD1022 L17-L21 — AC Series Circuits]] — RL, RC, RLC, resonance, power
- [[FAD1022 L22-L26 — Magnetism]] — Magnetic fields, Lorentz force, Ampere's law
- [[FAD1022 L31-L33 — Inductance & Transformers]] — Self/mutual induction, transformers
- [[FAD1022 Quick Quiz 2026 — Inductance & Transformers]] — Classroom quiz on mutual inductance & transformers
- [[FAD1022 L34-L38 — Semiconductors & Op-Amps]] — BJT biasing, op-amp configs
- [[FAD1022 L39-L42 — Atomic & Nuclear Physics]] — Bohr model, binding energy, radioactivity
- [[FAD1022 L43 — Modern Physics]] — Wave-particle duality, blackbody radiation
- [[FAD1022 L44 — Photons and Photoelectric Effect]] — Photon properties, photoelectric effect
- [[FAD1022 L45 — Introduction to Quantum Mechanics]] — Wave functions, Schrödinger equation, particle in a box
